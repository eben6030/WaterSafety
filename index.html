Water Safety Awareness

⚠️

Drowning Prevention

Always supervise children near water and wear life jackets. Be mindful of sudden changes in water depth.

🛟

Emergency Response

Learn CPR and always have a safety plan when near water. Keep a rescue floatation device handy.

🛡️

Safe Swimming

Swim in designated areas with lifeguards and avoid strong currents. Never swim alone.

🌊

Rip Currents

If caught in a rip current, swim parallel to the shore until you escape, then swim back to land.

🚤

Boating Safety

Always wear a life jacket when boating. Follow navigation rules and be aware of weather conditions.
